LessonOS

Simple lesson management for training businesses

LessonOS helps music schools, martial arts studios, tutoring centers, and other training businesses manage their students, classes, attendance, and leave requests — all from your phone.

Student Management

Track students, contact info, credit balances, and enrollment status.

Class Scheduling

Set up recurring weekly classes with day-of-week and time scheduling.

Quick Check-In

One-tap attendance check-in with automatic credit deduction.

Leave Management

Handle leave requests with approval workflows.

Google Drive Backup

Back up your data to your personal Google Drive for safekeeping.

CSV Export

Export student lists, attendance, and rosters to spreadsheets.

Available On
